About The Book

Synopsis

The book goes through a typical baby's day from sunrise to sunset showing how babies respond to situations differently through rhythmic text and playful illustrations.

Target Audience

A perfect gift for anyone bringing home a baby, whose cries may bewilder a parent, sibling, grandparent or any loved one learning the personality of the newest member of the family. Reading age 0-3 years.

Know More About The Author

Simrin Nair

Simrin Nair is a software engineer whose journey into motherhood has sparked her creativity. All Babies Cry is her debut children's picture book. The book is inspired by her son Krish who fights sleep with all his might. She knows that his strong will, while exhausting right now, is a trait that should be celebrated and makes him who he is. She hopes to relate to and connect with other parents through her writing. Originally from Canada, Simrin currently lives in California with her husband (and number one supporter) Vishnu and her son Krish.
